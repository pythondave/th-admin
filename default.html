Working Processes

All working processes are a work in progress. Nothing is set in stone.

Milestones of a TH sprint
  1. End of phase 1: we have a prototype which illustrates the idea of what we're going to do on this sprint, without going into too much detail
  2. End of phase 2: we have a well-built prototype which does everything we wanted/want in this sprint
  3. End of phase 3: we have a live system which does everything we wanted/want in this sprint (including any backend stuff)

Each sprint will be different, and things will be kept flexible - particularly as we learn more about what works best.

People of a TH sprint
  • Phase 1: AT/AR and RR
  • Towards end of phase 1: share everything with everyone so any potential issues can be raised, and we can plan the remaining phases
  • Phase 2: mainly RR
  • Phase 3: mainly OV (note that work on backend stuff can sometimes begin after the end of phase 1)
